# Privacy Policy for TaasClub

**Last Updated:** December 8, 2025

## Introduction

TaasClub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
- **Profile Information:** Optional profile picture and username.
- **In-App Purchases:** When you purchase Diamonds, we receive transaction confirmation from Google Play/App Store (we do not store payment card details).

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** Game statistics, room codes joined, gameplay patterns.
- **Log Data:** Error logs, crash reports, performance data.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Real Money Transactions:** TaasClub does not process real-money payments between users.
- **Location Data:** We do not collect precise GPS location.
- **Contacts:** We do not access your phone contacts.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the TaasClub service
- Process in-app purchases (Diamonds)
- Improve game experience and fix bugs
- Detect and prevent fraud or cheating
- Send important service announcements

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT** sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers:** Firebase (Google), RevenueCat for app functionality
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers:** In case of merger or acquisition

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure Firebase authentication
- Regular security audits

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Delete:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics in app settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@taasclub.app

## Children's Privacy

TaasClub is intended for users **18 years of age or older**. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses:

- **Firebase (Google):** Authentication, database, analytics
- **RevenueCat:** In-app purchase management
- **LiveKit:** Voice/video chat (optional feature)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
