## Turn Flow

```
1. DRAW: Deck OR Discard (if Visited)
2. DISCARD: Play 1 card
   -OR- VISIT: Show 3 pure sequences
   -OR- DECLARE: End game
```

---

## Visiting Requirements

Choose ONE:
- **3 Pure Sequences** (no wilds)
- **7 Dublees** (pairs)
- **3 Tunnels** (instant win!)

---
